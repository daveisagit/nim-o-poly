Rules

Basic Play

Last player to add a cell wins, if you can't go then you've lost.

Stakes Variation

Play stops when the target number of cells is reached (or, if play can't continue).
The first player to reach the target wins.
If the target is unreachable then as in basic play whoever played last wins.

If Player A goes first, then Player B chooses the initial target (vice versa).
The target may increase during play as follows.

On their turn a player may have the option to increase the target by 1 before laying a new cell.

The right to be able to do so, is like using the doubling dice in backgammon.
Initially, any player has the right to increase the target by 1.
Subsequently, the right to increase the target by 1 alternates.
Unlike backgammon there is no option to decline the increase.

The winners reward is a stake value equal to the target when the game stops.
